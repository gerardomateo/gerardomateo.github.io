Gerardo Mateo

Gerardo is the Head of Software Research & Development (R&D) at GLASS — a Silicon Valley-based software ecosystem company powering high-performing governments. He's part of the founding team that took the company from $0 to more than $3MM in revenue. He's leading R&D efforts to unlock Artificial Intelligence (AI) algorithms to optimize governments’ operational processes to make decisions with minimal human intervention.

He's an Engineer with a B. Eng. in Computer Science & Technology, and an Organization of American States (OAS) scholar. He's led the development of high-impact global initiatives and projects in the fields of technology and innovation in Germany, the U.S. and the Dominican Republic, specializing in the development of prototypes and digital tools to simplify manual processes within complex organizations.
