Gerardo Mateo

Gerardo Mateo is a Forbes Under 30 Awardee, Tech Entrepreneur, and Engineer. He is the Director of Platforms and a Founding Team Member at Glass, a Silicon Valley startup revolutionizing government procurement through its G-Commerce platform, which simplifies small purchases without bids or excessive paperwork, a market valued at $800 billion in the U.S.

He holds a B. Eng. in Computer Science and Technology from CUFE in Beijing and is a scholar of the Organization of American States (OAS), Tianjin University, and Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich’s Global Entrepreneurship School.

Gerardo's work has been featured by the World Trade Organization, where he was a High-Level Speaker at the WTO Public Forum 2024. His achievements have also been recognized by the U.S. Embassy in the Dominican Republic and the International Forum for Young Entrepreneurs of the Caribbean. He also served on Youth Service America’s Board of Directors, becoming one of its youngest appointed members.
